READ ME for the Player_Retention_Engagement project

Business Questions: What drives revenue? How concentrated is monetization? Can we predict high-value users?

Key Findings--
Payer Rate = 95.47%
ARPU = $97.97
ARPPU = $102.62
Top 5% of users generate 72.33% of total revenue
High-value users (“Whales”) purchase on average 15.39 days after install
Session frequency strongly correlates with LTV (r = 0.035)
iOS users show higher monetization than Android
  (
  Android     90.748913
  iOS        105.617961
  )


Product recommendations--
Encourage Early First Purchase (Within 7 Days)
  Ex: Time-limited starter bundles
Increase Session Frequency via Reward Loops
  Ex: Daily login rewards
Focus Monetization Efforts on High-Engagement Genres
  Ex: Double down on genres with higher ARPU.
Create Dolphin-Targeted Upsell Bundles
  Ex: Offer: Mid-tier bundles. Subscription perks. Upgrade incentives.
